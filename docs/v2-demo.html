07 · cards

Cards

White surface, 1 px subtle border, 8 px radius, navy-tinted --shadow-sm. Hover lifts to --shadow-md.

Plain card

Surface, border, subtle shadow. The everyday container for grouped content.

Elevated card

Hover or sticky surfaces lift to --shadow-md. Same border, deeper navy tint.

Linked card ↗

Click-through cards add a chevron and elevate on hover. Avoid the "card-as-button" anti-pattern unless the whole tile is the action.
